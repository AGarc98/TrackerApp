The Short Version

PrivateLift does not collect your data. Everything you enter — your workouts, sessions,
body measurements, and progress photos — is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing
is sent to us, to a cloud server, or to any third party. We have no servers that hold
your information because we have no way to receive it in the first place.

1. Who We Are
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PrivateLift is an independent fitness tracking application. For questions about this

2. What Data the App Stores — and Where
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PrivateLift stores all of the following locally on your device in a SQLite database.
This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or sync it
(see Sections 4 and 5).

  Training Data
    - Exercises you create or edit (name, type, muscle groups, rest duration)
    - Workouts and routines you build
    - Session logs: dates, sets, reps, weight, distance, time, and notes
    - Per-session perceived exertion (RPE) ratings

  Body Measurements
    - Body weight entries
    - Body fat percentage entries (optional)
    - Progress photos, if you choose to save them. Photos are stored as files on your
      device's local filesystem and are never transmitted anywhere.

  Preferences
    - Your display name (optional)
    - App settings: weight unit, theme, rest timer configuration, and similar preferences
    - Active routine selection

We do not collect any information that could identify you as a person — no name tied
to an account, no email address, no device identifiers, and no location data.

3. What We Do NOT Collect
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of the following:

  - Your name, email address, or any account credentials
    (PrivateLift has no account system and requires no sign-up)
  - Your location or GPS data
  - Your device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, Android Advertising ID, or similar)
  - Usage analytics or behavioral tracking of any kind
  - Crash reports sent to a remote server
  - Any data for advertising purposes
  - Any biometric data beyond what you voluntarily enter into the app

4. Optional Feature: Calendar Sync
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PrivateLift can optionally create calendar events in your device's system calendar when
you finish a training session. This feature is:

  - Disabled by default
  - Only activated if you explicitly turn it on in Settings
  - Requires your permission to access the system calendar, which your device will
    prompt you to grant

When enabled, PrivateLift writes session events to your local system calendar. It does
not read any existing calendar events, and it does not sync your calendar data anywhere.
Your calendar data remains under the control of whatever calendar provider you use on
your device (e.g., Apple Calendar, Google Calendar). We are not responsible for how
those third-party providers handle your data.

You can disable Calendar Sync at any time in Settings.

5. Optional Feature: Desktop Sync
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PrivateLift offers an optional sync with the companion PrivateLift desktop application.
When used:

  - The connection is established exclusively over your local Wi-Fi network (LAN).
    No data is routed through the internet or any external server.
  - The sync is authenticated by a short-lived token that you manually enter or scan
    from your desktop. The token expires after each use.
  - Your training data is transferred directly from your phone to your own desktop
    computer and is stored only there.

This feature is entirely opt-in and requires you to own and operate the desktop
application. We never have access to data transmitted during a sync.

6. Data Export and Backup
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PrivateLift allows you to export your data as an encrypted backup archive and to import
it to restore your data. When you export:

  - The archive is saved to your device's local storage.
  - You may optionally encrypt it with a passkey of your choosing using AES-256 encryption.
  - We never receive, see, or have access to your export files.

You are responsible for the security of any backup files you create and where you
choose to store or transfer them.

7. Third-Party Services & SDKs
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PrivateLift does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms,
or data brokers. We do not use any SDKs that collect data about you or your device for
purposes beyond the core functionality of the app.

The app is distributed through the Apple App Store and/or Google Play Store. Those
platforms may collect their own data (such as download counts and crash reports) under
their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Apple's and Google's privacy
policies separately.

8. Children's Privacy
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PrivateLift is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of
digital consent in your jurisdiction). Because the app does not collect any personal
data from anyone, it does not knowingly collect data from children either. If you are
under 13, please do not use this app without parental supervision.

9. Your Rights and Data Deletion
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Because all your data is stored locally on your own device, you are in complete control
of it at all times. You can:

  - View all your data within the app at any time
  - Export your data at any time from Settings
  - Delete individual sessions, exercises, routines, or biometric entries from within
    the app
  - Delete all data by uninstalling the app or using the "Restore" function with a
    blank state

We hold no copies of your data, so there is nothing for us to delete on our end. If you
uninstall the app and delete any backup files you created, your data is gone entirely.
