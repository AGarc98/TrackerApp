FULL DESCRIPTION

Private Lift is a serious training tool built for people who care about both their
performance and their privacy. Every set, session, and milestone you log stays on
your device — no account required, no subscription, no data harvested.

Whether you're running a 12-week powerlifting block, managing a custom PPL split, or
just trying to train consistently, Private Lift gives you the structure to build a
real program and the data to know whether it's working.

──────────────────────────────────────────
BUILD YOUR PROGRAM, YOUR WAY
──────────────────────────────────────────

Design training programs that match how you actually train — not how an app forces you
to think about training.

  Weekly Routines — Map workouts to specific days. Mix A and B weeks for programs that
  alternate between two schedules. Set which day your training week starts.

  Async Routines — Build a sequential queue instead of a day schedule. Work through
  it at your own pace. Every time you complete the full sequence, that's one cycle.
  Ideal for athletes whose training days shift week to week.

  Workout Builder — Assemble any combination of exercises into a named workout day.
  Set target sets, reps, weight, time, or distance per exercise. Group exercises into
  supersets. Override the default rest period per exercise slot.

  Exercise Library — A pre-loaded library of common exercises gets you started
  immediately. Add your own, assign primary and secondary muscle groups, set a default
  rest duration, and categorize by type: strength, bodyweight, endurance, isometric,
  or weighted bodyweight.

──────────────────────────────────────────
A SESSION ENGINE BUILT FOR THE GYM FLOOR
──────────────────────────────────────────

Private Lift is designed to get out of your way between sets.

  Smart Rest Timer — Starts automatically when you log a set. Each exercise uses its
  own rest period, or falls back to your global default. Sound and haptic alerts tell
  you when to go again — you don't need to look at your phone.

  Exercise Swaps — Need to substitute something? Swap any exercise for this session
  only, without touching your program. Or swap the entire workout with another day in
  your routine when life gets in the way.

  Set Types — Log warm-up sets, working sets, drop sets, and failure sets. Warm-ups
  are tracked but kept separate from your performance data so they don't skew your
  numbers.

  Session Notes & RPE — Rate how hard the session felt and add notes at the end.
  Useful for spotting patterns you can't see in the numbers alone.

  Crash Recovery — The app saves your progress continuously. If it crashes or you
  accidentally close it mid-session, your sets are waiting for you when you reopen it.

──────────────────────────────────────────
ANALYTICS THAT MEAN SOMETHING
──────────────────────────────────────────

Understand your training, not just your numbers.

  Filter by any of 35 muscle groups organized into anatomical categories. Drill down
  to a single exercise. Select a 4, 8, 12, or 24-week window.

  See how your training volume, estimated 1-rep max, average load, distance, time, and
  session RPE have changed compared to the previous period of the same length.

  Primary and secondary volume are tracked separately — so when you bench press, you
  see the chest work and the tricep contribution distinctly.

──────────────────────────────────────────
YOUR DATA, COMPLETELY
──────────────────────────────────────────

  No Account — Private Lift works the moment you download it. There is no sign-up,
  no login, and no profile tied to a server anywhere.

  No Cloud — Your data lives in a local database on your device. We have no servers
  that receive your information because the app never sends it.

  No Subscription — Pay once, own it. No monthly fees, no features locked behind
  a tier.

  Export Anytime — Back up your full training history as an encrypted archive whenever
  you want. Your data is yours to keep and move.

  Desktop Companion (Coming Soon) — A companion desktop app will let you sync your
  training history to your own computer over your local Wi-Fi — no internet required.
  Review your data on a larger screen, manage multiple profiles, and keep a permanent
  archive of your entire training history.

──────────────────────────────────────────
WHO IS PRIVATE LIFT FOR?
──────────────────────────────────────────

Private Lift is built for lifters who want more than a basic workout log but don't
want to hand their health data to a company running it through a recommendation
algorithm. It suits:

  — Intermediate to advanced lifters running structured periodized programs
  — Coaches who want a no-frills tool to build and track client programs
  — Anyone who has looked at the permissions or subscription price of a mainstream
    fitness app and decided there has to be a better option

If you log every session, care about your numbers, and want to own your data — this
app was built for you.
